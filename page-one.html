Beaches

Cape Town's beaches boast stunning landscapes, from iconic Clifton to family-friendly Muizenberg, offering diverse experiences, breathtaking views, and vibrant atmospheres.

Top 6 Beaches

Camps Bay Beach

Camps Bay offers chic ambiance with golden sands, clear waters, and the Twelve Apostles backdrop. Trendy cafes and a lively atmosphere make it a social hotspot.

Clifton Beach

Four intimate beaches between boulders, each with white sands and clear waters. Popular for sunbathing, beach volleyball, and stunning sunset picnics in a picturesque setting.

Llandudno Beach

Secluded gem with powerful waves and golden sands, drawing surfers. The absence of amenities enhances its unspoiled beauty, offering a peaceful escape by the sea.

Muizenberg Beach

Long sandy stretch with colorful beach huts, ideal for novice surfers. A lively atmosphere with surf schools and cafes along the promenade creates a vibrant seaside experience.

Noordhoek Beach

Tranquil 8-kilometer stretch surrounded by dunes and mountains. Perfect for long walks and horseback riding, providing a serene escape and showcasing natural coastal beauty.

Boulders Beach

Famous for African penguins, Boulders Beach features granite boulders and a family-friendly swimming area. A unique and picturesque destination with soft sandy beaches and wildlife encounters.

Best Beaches For...

Dog Walks

Llandudno Beach in Cape Town is ideal for dog walking, with its pristine sands and relaxed atmosphere, providing a perfect setting.

Surfing

Muizenberg Beach is renowned for its consistent waves, making it the best spot for surfing in Cape Town with a vibrant surf culture.

Beach Town

Hermanus, a charming beach town near Cape Town, is celebrated for its scenic beauty, whale watching, and relaxed coastal atmosphere.

Seafood

Kalk Bay in Cape Town is a seafood lover's paradise, featuring diverse restaurants offering fresh catches with stunning ocean views.

Swimmimg

Camps Bay Beach in Cape Town is an ideal destination for swimming, with its clear waters, sandy shores, and stunning backdrop.

Fishing

Strandfontein Beach in Cape Town is favored by fishing enthusiasts, providing a serene setting and opportunities to catch a variety of fish.
