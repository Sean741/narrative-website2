Trails

Cape Town's trails offer stunning views of Table Mountain, the seashore, and the natural world.

Top 6 Trails/Hikes

Platteklip Gorge Trail

Take on the strenuous path to reach the famous top, where you'll be rewarded with expansive views of Cape Town and the Atlantic Ocean.

Spiral Route

Experience an exhilarating spiral ascent on this well-known summit, which offers unmatched views of the city, the ocean, and the surrounding countryside.

Cape of Good Hope Trail

Travel across a variety of terrain to reach Africa's southernmost point, where the Atlantic and Indian oceans converge amid spectacular rocks.

Elephants Eye Cave Trail

Explore verdant surroundings and uncover a cave that resembles an elephant's eye while taking in breathtaking views of the neighbouring mountains..

Skeleton Gorge Trail

Start at the botanical haven of Kirstenbosch and make your way up through verdant woodlands to Skeleton Gorge, to the highest point on Table Mountain.

Saddle Trail

Start at the botanical haven of Kirstenbosch and make your way up through verdant woodlands to Skeleton Gorge, to the highest point on Table Mountain.

So Where Are They?

While some trails are relatively close to the city center, others are a bit farther away, often within a short driving distance. Cape Town's natural surroundings provide a diverse range of trail options.
