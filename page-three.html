Attractions

Cape Town offers up some incredibly differce and unique attractions from the historic landmarks of Robben Island and the Castle of Good Hope to some breathtaking views of the city from a cable car. It truly has something for everyone.

Robben Island Prison

A historic prison tour revealing Nelson Mandela's struggles for freedom and justice.

Castle Of Good Hope

A historic fort in Cape Town, South Africa, oldest colonial building.

Cape Town Stadium

A modern stadium in Cape Town, South Africa, hosts sports and events.

Bo-Kaap neighborhood

Brightly colored houses in Cape Town's Bo-Kaap neighborhood, South Africa.

Table Mountain Cable Cars

Cable cars ascend Table Mountain, offering panoramic views of Cape Town.
