Wine Routes

The wine routes of Cape Town are well known for their stunning scenery, ancient vineyards, and superb wines.

So Where Are They?

While some trails are relatively close to the city center, others are a bit farther away, often within a short driving distance. Cape Town's natural surroundings provide a diverse range of trail options.

Cape Town Wines

Chenin Blanc

Cape Town's Chenin Blanc: Versatile, crisp white wine with refreshing acidity, showcasing diverse flavors beautifully.

Pinotage

Known for its unusual profile, Pinotage is a bold red wine from South Africa with deep colours, intense flavours, and unique character.

Shiraz

South African expressiveness is a full-bodied red wine with complex layers, spice, and robust fruit.

Sauvignon Blanc

Crisp Cape Town white, vibrant acidity, tropical fruit aromas, and refreshing herbaceous notes abound.
